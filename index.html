About the Atlas

As you know, Burning Man is a magical place that gets the best of human creativity and collaboration to flourish. To further understand what makes this magic happen, we are creating the first ever Black Rock Atlas - a map of the social patterns and networks that exist on the playa.

To do this, we are evolving the famous Six Degrees of Separation experiment by tracking the journey of vessels through the gift economy of Burning Man with technology and generative photography.

Join Us

Team

The Black Rock Atlas Team is a team of artists, designers and researchers based out of the Scalable Cooperation group at the MIT Media Lab. We are fascinated with questions about how groups of people come together to solve large-scale, difficult problems - and in particular how people can use technology and design to aid in their problem solving.

The team is comprised of Ziv Epstein, Micah Epstein, Christian Almenar, Matt Groh, Niccolo Pescetelli, Nick Obradovich, Manuel Cebrian and Iyad Rahwan.
